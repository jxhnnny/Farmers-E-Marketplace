How Our Marketplace Works

Simple steps to connect farmers and buyers

1. Register

Create your account as a farmer or buyer. It's free and takes just minutes.

2. List or Browse

Farmers list their produce. Buyers browse and search for what they need.

3. Connect & Negotiate

Direct communication between farmers and buyers for price negotiation.

4. Complete Transaction

Finalize the deal and arrange delivery or pickup. Both parties benefit.
